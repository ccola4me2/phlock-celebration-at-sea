Everything you need to know before booking the 2027 International Parrothead Day Cruise.

We sail June 26 – July 3, 2027 — 7 nights round-trip from Miami, Florida aboard the Margaritaville at Sea Beachcomber, with stops in Key West, George Town (Grand Cayman), and Ocho Rios (Jamaica).

Your fare includes your stateroom, main dining, and access to all Parrothead group VIP events, including the commemorative welcome package and "Come Monday" brunch. Gratuities, beverage packages, specialty dining, and travel insurance are optional add-ons — see our Cabins & Pricing page for details.
