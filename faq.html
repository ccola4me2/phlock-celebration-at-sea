FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking the 2028 International Parrothead Day Cruise.

Your fare includes your cabin, taxes and port charges, and access to all Parrothead group VIP events, including the commemorative lanyard and "Come Monday" brunch. Gratuities, beverage packages, and travel insurance are optional add-ons — see our Pricing page for details.

Reach out through our Contact page or call our team directly. We'll walk you through cabin availability and secure your spot with a deposit.

A deposit is required to reserve your cabin, with final payment due ahead of sailing. Exact amounts and deadlines for the 2028 sailing will be confirmed once the ship and date are announced — your travel host will provide the full schedule at booking.

Cancellation terms follow the cruise line's standard policy, with refund eligibility depending on how far in advance you cancel. We strongly recommend adding travel protection at booking to help cover unexpected cancellations.

Passport requirements depend on the itinerary and departure port for the 2028 sailing. We recommend traveling with a valid passport book whenever visiting international ports, and your travel host will confirm specific requirements once the itinerary is finalized.

Travel protection is optional but strongly recommended for all guests. It can help cover trip cancellation, delays, and medical needs while traveling.

We aim to bring back the live music and VIP events our group loves, but performers, activities, and schedules are subject to change and will be confirmed closer to sailing.

Our team is happy to help — visit the Meet Your Hosts page for direct contact info, or reach out through our Contact page.
