Giving Back

Cruising for Change

QUIETLY MAKING NOISE ON THE HIGH SEAS

Every sailing of the International Parrothead Day Cruise supports Singing for Change — a foundation built on the belief that doing good doesn't require a spotlight. We raise funds through our onboard Silent Auction and donate directly to a charity that puts money back into communities at the grassroots level through grant applications.

Visit Singing for Change
Our Charity Partner

Why Singing for Change?

Singing for Change was founded on a simple idea: $1 from every Jimmy Buffett concert ticket goes to fund community grants. No big fundraising galas. No self-promotion. Just quietly making noise. Their staff of one reached out when our donation arrived because it was larger than what they typically receive — a reminder of how much a passionate group of Parrotheads can accomplish.

"Have fun, make money, and leave the world a better place."— Jimmy Buffett
